Banana Pudding

Learn how to prepare your new favorite dessert dish!

As an avid fan of bananas, this banana pudding has been a favorite from childhood through adulthood.

It's the perfect homemade dessert for anyone to learn and share with their loved ones.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 bananas, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 (12 ounce) package vanilla wafer cookies

Steps

  1. Combine sugar and flour together in medium saucepan. Add eggs and milk and continue to stir over low heat.
  2. When the mixture has thickened, remove from heat and continue to stir as it cools slightly. Stir in butter and vanilla extract until smooth.
  3. Pour mixture into serving dish and layer with banana slices and vanilla wafers.
  4. Chill at least 1 hour in the refrigerator before serving.

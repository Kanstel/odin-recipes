Old-Fashioned Chicken and Dumplings

The warm, cozy classic that you can't beat!

Chicken and dumplings are a family-favorite that never fails to please the whole table.

This recipe is one I encourage anyone to really play around with and make your own additions to, such as your favorite veggies!

Ingredients

  • 2 (10.5 ounce) cans condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 3 (14 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 3 cups shredded cooked chicken meat
  • 2 (10 ounce) cans refrigerated biscuit dough

Steps

  1. Stir condensed soup, chicken broth, and shredded chicken together in a large saucepan over medium-high heat until it begins to simmer.
  2. Cut each biscuit into quarters, and gently stir into the soup.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until biscuits are no longer doughy in the center. (10-15 minutes)
  4. Season as preferred, serve, and enjoy!
